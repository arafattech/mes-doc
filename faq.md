## General Questions

### What is Hybritech MES?

Hybritech MES (Manufacturing Execution System) is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline manufacturing and commercial operations. It provides real-time visibility, workflow automation, and data-driven insights across multiple business functions.

### Who can use this system?

The system is designed for:
- Manufacturing managers and supervisors
- Sales and commercial teams
- Quality assurance personnel
- HR and administrative staff
- Executive management

### How do I get access?

Contact your system administrator or IT department to request access credentials. Access levels are assigned based on your role and responsibilities.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in. What should I do?

1. Verify your username and password
2. Check if Caps Lock is on
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Contact IT support if the issue persists

### The page is loading slowly

Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try a different browser

### I don't see a module I need

Module visibility depends on your user role and permissions. Contact your administrator to request access to additional modules.
