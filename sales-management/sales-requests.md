# Sales Requests

**Sales Requests** tracks incoming inquiries and preliminary requests from potential clients before they become official orders.

## Workflow
1.  **Receive Request**: New requests appear in the list with a status of 'New'.
2.  **Review**: Click to view the details of the customer's needs.
3.  **Action**:
    *   **Approve**: Convert the request into a formal **Sales Order**.
    *   **Reject**: Mark the request as invalid or unfulfillable.
